# Change Management
Production changes are documented, approved, tested, and deployed under the change management process before being released. This control ensures that unauthorised or untested changes cannot reach production and creates an auditable record of who approved what, when, and against which policy.

## Control Attributes
* Every production change has a corresponding change request that documents scope, risk classification, and rollback plan
* Production changes are approved by an authorised change approver prior to deployment
* Pre-deployment testing is completed and evidence retained before release
